SAFETY & COMPLIANCE

Our Commitment

Safety

At Blue Life Labor, our steadfast commitment to employee safety is fundamental. We prioritize the health, well-being, and security of our team in every aspect of operations. Our business is fully committed to ensuring the safety and well-being of our employees through education and training on workplace safety. We ensure our employees are regularly updated on best practices and protocols to maintain a secure and informed work environment.

We provide training on the following safety protocols to ensure a secure and compliant workplace.

Our Safety Protocols:
  • Ag WorkSafe
  • Ergonomics
  • Pesticide & Chemical handling
  • Emergency First Aid
  • Field Sanitation
  • Cold Stress/Heat Safety
  • Proper Use of Tools and Equipment
  • Personal Protections Equipment and more
Compliance

Blue Life Labor is committed to maintaining full compliance with all state and federal laws and regulations, ensuring we uphold our reputation for integrity and responsibility. Our operations are transparent, ensuring clear communication and accountability at every level.

We comply with:
  • U.S. Department of Labor
  • Oregon Bureau of Labor & Industries
  • U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA)
  • Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA)
  • SAIF Corporation
