About Us:

Blue Life Labor Contractor Corp was founded by Jonathan Castillo in 2022, working alongside his lead foreman Benjamin Santiago representing the union between farming and finance. Deeply rooted in the agriculture industry of the Pacific Northwest, Benjamin developed his expertise over a 20-year career as agricultural foreman at E&S Farms in Woodburn, Oregon. Jonathan earned his bachelor's degree in Finance at Oregon State University, specializing in business operations. Together, with our combined on-the-fields experience and financial and legal expertise, Blue Life Labor delivers safe and high-quality labor that growers can depend on.

Here at Blue Life Labor, we strive to provide the finest farm labor to deliver the finest results. Alongside the exceptional farm labor that we provide, we also guarantee meticulous and compliant record-keeping, ensuring that all documentation meets regulatory standards and is accurately maintained for transparency and accountability. We take pride in the high level of professionalism maintained by our back office, ensuring that every aspect of our operations is handled with precision, integrity, and expertise. All of our payroll and bookkeeping processes are handled in-house, allowing us to provide fast and accurate reporting, ensuring timely and efficient financial management. This allows us to instantly deliver real-time information right to your hands whenever you need it.
